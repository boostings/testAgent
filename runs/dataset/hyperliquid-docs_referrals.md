### 

How do I use a referral code?

Enter a referral code on <https://app.hyperliquid.xyz/referrals>[](https://app.hyperliquid.xyz/referrals) or use a friend's referral link. Using a referral code will give you a 4% discount on your fees for your first $25M in volume. Referral discounts do not apply to vaults or sub-accounts because those are treated as independent accounts in the clearinghouse.
